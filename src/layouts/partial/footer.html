OUR LOCATION

109 State Ave NE
Olympia, WA 98501

We're located in the heart of downtown Olympia in the old Olympia Press Building, on the corner of State and Capital. There are many entrances to the Olympia Press Building, So when you arrive for your booking appointment, please make sure you are waiting at the front door on the State Avenue side of the building. If you get lost or feel that your waiting in the wrong spot, please call (360) 930-9686 for assistance.

PARKING

There is plenty of metered parking spots available on the street just outside our front door. Parking is free on weekdays after 5:00pm and all day Saturday through Sunday.

Downtown Olympia can be busy on weekends, so please allow yourself plenty of time to locate a parking spot. If your having trouble finding a free spot, parking lots with day rates are available across the street.
